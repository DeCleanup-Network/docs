# Intro

Decentralized Clenaup Network represents a revolutionary approach to environmental action, combining blockchain technology with community-driven initiatives to create tangible, real-world impact. By tokenizing cleanup efforts, DeCleanup empowers individuals and groups to take meaningful steps toward a cleaner planet while being rewarded for their contributions.
